The target audience of this website will be community members looking for information about the local businesses so they can know where to go to. They might be looking to promote their own business and want to join the Chamber of Commerce. Somebody visiting this website might be a parent looking for an event to go to for the weekend. Another audience member might be a resident that wants to look at the weather along with upcoming community announcements all in one place.

Rebecca Williams

Rebecca is a mother of 4 children. Rebecca loves the community and wants to have good bonding time with her family. Rebecca can find out about these community events by visiting the "discover" page on the Show Low City Chamber of Commerce page. Now she knows where to go and has fun plans for her family for the weekend.

Craig Barnett

Craig Barnett owns his own local cabinetry company. He wants to promote his business to people in the community that are building and need cabinetry installed. Craig can visit the Show Low City Chamber join page so that he can learn more about the benefits of joining the chamber and be able to fill out a membership applicaton.
